Help Fund Smooth UI

There are a lot of great tools out there for creating UI mockups and wireframes, but these applications are designed for a mouse and keyboard. With the explosion in popularity of tablets, hybrid laptops, and touch monitors there is a need for applications that make touch a first-class citizen. UI mockups are a perfect fit for this move to touch. We can give UI designers the ability to quickly create mockups on their touch device just like they would in a sketchbook, with all of the power and flexibility of a feature-rich web application.

So what are the features, you ask? Here is what is currently in the works:

  • Saving and sharing of projects
  • Test your designs with transitions between pages
  • A library of ready to use templates, components, and icons

FAQ

Why isn't this on Kickstarter or Indiegogo?
I started this project through the Startup Engineering class on Coursera, creating this crowdfunder was a part of the coursework.
Is $2,000 really enough to finish the development?
I will be continuing to work on this project regardless, but this is my first attempt at crowdfunding and raising $2,000 would be a huge encouragement and provide the incentive to dedicate more of my time towards development.
How can I report bugs or suggest features?
Feel free to email Andrew Clark, the developer of Smooth UI.
Is there any other way to donate?
Right now bitcoin is the only supported payment method, the reasoning here goes back to the Coursera startup class. Bitcoin is the easiest way for the instructors to rank projects by how much they have raised, since it is simple to look up transactions in the block chain.
